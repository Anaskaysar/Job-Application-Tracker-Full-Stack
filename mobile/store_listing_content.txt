Full Description:
JobTrackerr helps you manage your job search journey efficiently. 

Features:
• Track multiple job applications in one place
• Monitor interview stages and follow-ups
• Store offer details and important deadlines
• Set reminders for application responses
• Export data for backup and sharing
• Clean, intuitive interface for easy navigation
• Secure cloud sync across devices
• Customizable application statuses

Perfect for active job seekers, career changers, and anyone managing multiple job opportunities. Take control of your job search with JobTracker!

Download now and never miss an important deadline again.
